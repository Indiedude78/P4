DIY kit

Description

The Luminate DIY Kit is a great way to get started with your own DIY project. The kit includes all the parts you need to build your own Luminate LED Ambient lighting system.

What's included

  • 1x Luminate LED Strip
  • 1x Luminate Controller
  • 1x Luminate Power Supply
  • 1x Luminate Remote
  • 1x Luminate Mounting Kit
  • 1x Dupont Wires
  • 1x Instruction Manual
